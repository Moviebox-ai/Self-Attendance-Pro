Delete Your Account

Self Attendance Pro users can permanently delete their account and associated data. Follow the steps below to submit a deletion request.

How to delete your account

  1. 1
    Open the Self Attendance Pro app and sign in.
  2. 2
    Go to Settings → Delete Account.
  3. 3
    Confirm the deletion request.
  4. 4
    Your account and associated attendance data will be permanently deleted.
ℹ️ Data retention notice: Some data (if required by law or for security purposes) may be retained for a limited period before permanent deletion.
